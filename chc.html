Coffee House Club is a discussion group held on the first Sunday of every month in Whitehaven. We meet in the afternoon to explore & share ideas together with other smart planning and journalling people in a public place.

Coffee House Club is now running again with the old meeting format. Thanks for your patience with us.

We've had a wonderful time making friends and exploring new ideas over the years and hope to continue the trend where it left off.

Founded by David, Irina, Jack & Ahleen.
